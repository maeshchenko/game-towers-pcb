PCB TD — Privacy

Last updated: 2026-07-04

PCB TD is a browser game that runs entirely on your device. It has no accounts, no login, and no server that stores your data.

What is stored, and where

  • Game progress (campaign stars, unlocked levels, achievements, meta-upgrades, statistics, settings) is saved in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device and is only readable by the game itself.
  • You can wipe it any time from the campaign menu (Reset progress) or by clearing site data in your browser.

Cookies & tracking

The game sets no cookies and performs no advertising or cross-site tracking. No third-party trackers are embedded.

Analytics

The game may optionally record anonymous, aggregate gameplay events (e.g. "a level was completed") to help balance difficulty. If enabled, this is cookieless, contains no personal data, and cannot identify you. No such data is collected unless a privacy-preserving analytics endpoint is configured for the deployment.

Hosting

When played on a portal such as itch.io or GitHub Pages, that host may collect its own standard server logs (e.g. IP address, browser type) under its privacy policy — separate from the game itself.

Children

The game collects no personal information from anyone, including children.
